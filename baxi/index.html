Baxi

About Baxi

Baxi is a multifunctional Discord bot developed by Red_Wolf2467. It offers a variety of features, including:

  • Moderation: Automatic and manual moderation tools
  • Security: Protection against spam and abuse
  • Guard: Monitoring and safeguarding servers
  • Globalchat: Connect with other servers
  • Minigames: Various entertaining games
  • Music: Play and control music
  • Ticketing: Support for ticket systems

Features

Baxi offers a wide range of features to enhance and secure your Discord server:

  • Automated Moderation: Automatic bans, warnings, and actions against rule violations.
  • User Management: Manage roles, users, and permissions.
  • Security Measures: Protection against raid attacks, spam, and other threats.
  • Music Control: High-quality music playback from various platforms.
  • Interactive Minigames: Various games to entertain the community.
  • Ticket System: Easy management of support requests and issues.
